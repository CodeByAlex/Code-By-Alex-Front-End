Developing sites for User Inclusivity: a talk about web accessibility

Gave a talk about accessinbility for the CharmCityJS meetup group in Baltimore, MD. The talk covered the reasons why developers should strive to meet Accessibility Standards, such as WCAG. Testing Tools and best development practices were descibed with several real-world examples. I then went over the business value for adding the necessary features so that the members of the audience could return to their jobs with ways to backup the process change.
